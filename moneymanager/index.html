Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Money Manager: Expense Tracker is a personal finance tracking app designed to help users record and review their income, expenses, transfers, accounts, categories, budgets, and financial activity.

Information We Collect

Money Manager does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with us or third parties. The financial information entered by users, including transactions, accounts, categories, budgets, notes, and descriptions, is stored locally on the user's device.

Local Storage

The app stores user-created financial data on the device using local app storage. This data is not uploaded to any server controlled by us.

CSV Export

The app may allow users to export their transaction data as a CSV file. CSV export is initiated by the user, and the exported file is saved to the user's selected storage location or downloads folder.

No Financial Advice

Money Manager is a personal finance tracking tool. It does not provide banking, lending, investment, tax, insurance, or financial advice.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their app data by deleting records inside the app, clearing app storage, or uninstalling the app from their device.

Children's Privacy

Money Manager is intended for users aged 18 and above. The app is not directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.

Contact
